BORED? WE CAN FIX THAT

  • Come and see the great ice sheets of Europa! Camp out under the stars in our specialized sub-arctic tents and watch for ice geysers which burst up periodically from Europa's icy surface. Try exotic dishes made from the aquatic life native to the frigid oceans beneath Europa, or fish for your own! Our tour guides are specially trained and can take you on wondrous expeditions into the caves and glacial tunnels beneath Europa's surface. Europa is the perfect choice for those looking for an icy adventure.

  • The kind Uudf'n people of the planet of Teegarden's Star C have graciously opened a resort in their city of Mzzixpa and are eager to meet you! They offer a broad variety of activities to immerse you in their vibrant culture. Try their traditional Oadfl dance lessons, adapted for those with two arms and two legs. Take tours led by Uudf'n tour guides to their famous landmarks and learn their cultural history. Several restaurants open around the resort are available for experiencing authentic Uudf'n cuisine, which is safely adjusted for human consumption. Each visit comes with a complimentary brochure informing you of Uudf'n cultural norms and expectations to ensure your visit is enjoyable both for you and our amazing hosts.

  • TRAPPIST-1e offers many opportunities for spelunking and rock-climbing due to its extensive cave systems and high canyon walls. Its high sun exposure and strong ozone layer also make it one of our favorite resorts for swimming and sunbathing. Try our unique sand sculpture courses so you can impress your friends back home!
