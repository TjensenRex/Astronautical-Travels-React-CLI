About Us

In 2018 SpaceX was making market-shaking advancements in rocket technology. Their influence spurred on a rivival of space-travel companies. Our origins lie within SpaceX. Our founders were head engineers at SpaceX, directly responsible for some of the more notable advancements in rocket technology. In late 2018, these few engineers proposed a new direction for the company- space travel. The proposal made its way to Elon Musk himself, who vehemently rejected it. This team of individuals bound together and left SpaceX with vivid dreams of new planets and destinations with awesome tourist activities.

We are Astronautical Travels. Come expand the edges of the final frontier with us. Meet new peoples, species, and cultures. See incredible planets that no Earth-bound human has ever seen before. Experience gravity that pulls on you a little less, while sliding down the largest natural water slide in the galaxy!
